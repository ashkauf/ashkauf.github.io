About Me

Ashley Kaufman is a student at Indiana University where she is studying Informatics with a focus in cybersecurity. She particularly interested in the ethical uses of technology and the design and development of ethical technologies.

In her spare time, Ashley enjoys participating in Model United Nations and is heavily involved with her sorority, Delta Phi Epsilon. She is currently serving as the Vice President of Operations for the latter.
